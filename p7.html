Immersive Healthcare - Denture care

The Immersive Healthcare project is designed to create a risk-free training environment for healthcare personnel using AI Virtual Human co-workers and patients in VR. It uses AI analytics to provide objective, evidence-based assessment of skills and communication competency. The aim of this project is to help government, hospital, and long-term care center administration assess the performance competency of their teams.

Role

Art team lead

Software Used:

Unity, Maya, Substance Painter, Photoshop

Company

Virtro Technology

Process

As the art team lead on this project, I participated in the brainstorming of the environment design with the stakeholders and allocated the related tasks to our animators and modelers. The project was aimed at the Oculus Quest platform, so I took performance considerations into account from the beginning of the design process. We laid out the room in a way that the user wouldn't face any performance issues, such as draw calls, while still providing plenty of interactivity for the user. I also took part in creating the interaction design of the interactable props, We used a lot of visual and audio cues for the user to lead the user through the experience if the user is having trouble progressing.

Responsibilities

  • Designed user/prop interaction with visual and audio feedback
  • Create scene assets and environment
  • Managed and allocated tasks to team members and ensure tasks on completed within the set deadline by the project manager
  • Reviewed and optimized scene assets to meet the technical requirements
