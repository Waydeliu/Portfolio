IHS - Endoscopy Training simulation

The IHS Endoscopy Project is a joint development between Intelligent Haptronic Solutions and Virtro Technology to create surgical training simulation systems. The product uses medical data and a surgical AI training engine will provide students/ doctors with an immersive training platform together with a real-time haptic device.

Role

VR developer, 3d Artist

Software Used:

Unity, Maya, Substance painter, Photoshop

Company

Virtro Technology, Intelligent Haptronic Solutions

Responsibilities

  • Created and design the visual of the simulation with life-like model of human organs
  • Collaborated on creating high-level design of transforming in-game physic data into haptic data for the mechanical device
  • Research and setup the physic simulations with soft body, fluid, and cloth to achieve realistic flesh-like interaction and visual
  • Supported mechanical engineer linking device haptic data into Unity simulation
